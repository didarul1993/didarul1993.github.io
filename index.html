About Me

Hello, everyone! I am Didarul Islam, Ph.D., an Earth observation scientist specializing in multi-sensor remote sensing, GeoAI, and geospatial data science. My work integrates LiDAR, SAR, optical satellite imagery, machine learning, and scalable geospatial computing to support environmental monitoring, climate impact assessment, wildfire risk analysis, forest structure mapping, and coastal resilience.

I am passionate about transforming complex spatial data into decision-ready maps, models, and data products that support ecosystem monitoring, hazard assessment, climate adaptation, and sustainable environmental planning.

About Me

I am an Earth observation scientist and geospatial data scientist working at the intersection of remote sensing, GeoAI, spatial modeling, and environmental decision support. My work integrates LiDAR, SAR, optical satellite imagery, machine learning, and scalable geospatial computing to develop decision-ready maps, models, and data products.

My research and applied work focus on forest structure and biomass mapping, wildfire-treatment outcomes, Local Climate Zone mapping, agricultural forecasting, blue-carbon monitoring, climate vulnerability, and environmental risk assessment.
